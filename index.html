Hello!

My name is Eduardo Cardoso. I'm an artist/mail artist, musician and web developer.

I'm very fond of minimalism and my works, ranging from visual poetry to collage and mixed media, usually focus on simplicity and reduction of elements. Along the years I developed my own expression and some artists inside the Mail Art network call my style, not minimalism, but distillism, as I distill the works to their essential and sometimes to very basic shapes.

As a web developer I currently work with HTML + CSS and I'm slowly diving into Javascript.
